## Gold-Standard Checklist

- Every flagship card has either a real screenshot, a safe anonymised diagram, or a deliberately designed thumbnail.
- Public repos have aligned descriptions, topics, README summaries, and at least one visual proof point where relevant.
- Private systems are represented through safe architecture diagrams, outcome metrics, and anonymised case studies.
- The GitHub profile has six pinned repos matching the portfolio narrative.
- The Idea Store is checked at desktop and mobile widths after each visual change.
- The CV evidence log is updated monthly with measurable, anonymised outcomes.
